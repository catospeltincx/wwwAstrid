“This in Turn” is a dynamic dance performance in which five dancers explore the energy, excitement, and renewal of spring. During the performance, the dancers seek out a sense of collectivity, drawing the audience into a pulsating, circular flow of bodies—an energetic journey that carries you from within. Repetitive rhythms and circular movements make tangible how the individual merges into a larger whole: the group, the space, the moment. In This in Turn, the viewer is invited to collectively feel and experience this transition: the blossoming, the new beginning, the shared carrying of an energy that keeps flowing. The audience sits around the dancers, and what emerges is an intense and intimate experience in which dance, music, and audience are immersed in a single shared flow.
